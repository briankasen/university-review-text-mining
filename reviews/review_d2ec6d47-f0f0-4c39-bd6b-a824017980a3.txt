"UniversityOfIllinoisAtUrbanaChampaign The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign's mission states that it is: ""charged by our state to enhance the lives of citizens in Illinois, across the nation, and around the world through our leadership in learning, discovery, engagement, and economic development"" (""about,"" 2017). As a current sophomore attending, Urbana-Champaign as truly been a leader in all aspects. To say that this is the best school is biased on my part. but it is the truth. U of I has taken the time to mold me, and push me to excel. The opportunities they offer are mountainous, from having numerous majors to select from, different classes to enrich oneself, and establishments you can call your home. All persons on that campus want you to succeed. The advisors, professors, tutors, student aid, those who work in the office that may never see, board members, and the peers, students. The environment the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign provides is that of a campus that wants you to be the best you can."
